The Artifice Ecosystem runs on four systems — discover, connect, locate, archive — connected through the work. The infrastructure is not behind the scenes. It is the practice.

Four Systems
Discover Radar Discover practitioners, venues, and opportunities. The sensing layer — scanning for signals across the creative landscape.
Connect Profiles Contracted profiles, relationships, and IP. The network layer — linking talent with organizations and opportunities.
Locate Atlas Physical venues and their capabilities. The spatial network — every location indexed with context and capacity.
Archive Index Work + projects + programs, indexed with full provenance. Contains Artifacts (the work) and Worlds (the prototypes — URL + IRL).
